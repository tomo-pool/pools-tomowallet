Stake ownership

Stakers in here are receiving rewards in a decentralized way, no one, except you, is able to control your stake.

Resigning votes

The Company has no control over when a pool shall be resigned. Stakers shall use their staked TOMO amount to vote for whether the pool shall resign or not. If over 66% of the total staked TOMO is voted for resigning, the pool shall be resigned by any one.

Stake partition

The total staked TOMO in a pool must be divided into 2 parts:

  • Candidate Deposit: 50,000 TOMO
  • Candidate Vote: Any amount that exceeds 50,000 TOMO.

If a pool shall be resigned, the Candidate Deposit shall be locked for 30 days and the Candidate Vote shall be locked for 2 days. Two days after resigning, the amount of TOMO released to each staker is proportional to the total amount of TOMO the staker stakes in the pool.

No staking rewards if pool is not a masternode

The minimum staked TOMO for a pool to become a masternode is not fixed as it changes depending on the TomoChain blockchain state. In case a pool does not have enough vote/stake or the pool is not a masternode, no rewards would be distributed to stakers of that pool.

Annual Return of Interest (ROI) is not fixed
